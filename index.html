Common Questions

Is my financial data secure?
Yes. Your data is cryptographically secured. Only your authenticated user account can access your specific transactions. Even our AI only processes data on-the-fly and does not train on your personal finances.
Does it support UPI apps like PhonePe/GPay?
Spends-In is a manual tracker with AI assistance. You can create a "UPI" payment method. When you spend via GPay, just tell Sai "Spent 200 via UPI for recharge" and it logs it instantly.
How does the recurring feature work?
We run a secure automated system every hour. If you have a rent payment set for the 1st of every month, Spends-In automatically creates that transaction for you. It ensures you never miss a recurring expense.
